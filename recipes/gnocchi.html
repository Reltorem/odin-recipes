Gnocchi

Description

This creamy pesto gnocchi recipe is fried in spices to add extra flavour

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh gnocchi
  • 2 tsp pesto
  • Olive oil
  • Italian herbs and spices
  • 300g diced pumpkin
  • 200g diced sweet potato
  • 250ml cooking cream

Steps

  1. Put the pumpkin and sweet potato on a tray and bake at 200C for 20min.
  2. Meanwhile, fry the gnocchi in a pan with a generous drizzle of olive oil until brown
  3. Add herbs & spices, garlic and fry until fragrant
  4. Add cream and mix well
  5. Stir in baked vegetables and serve
