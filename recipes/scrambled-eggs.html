SCRAMMMMBLED EGGS

Description

This is a very easy dish that you can make for breakfast, lunch or dinner

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs (Or however many you want)
  • A dash of egg spices
  • A dash of milk
  • 1/2 tsp minced garlic (Optional)

Steps

  1. Break the eggs into a small bowl
  2. Add the milk and spices
  3. Whisk well, folding air into the mixture to make it light and fluffy
  4. Gently stir the eggs in the pan until it reaches desired texture. Don't overstir or overcook the eggs
  5. You can add some garlic to the pan before adding the eggs, if you wish
