Lasagna!

Description

This is a vegetable lasagna. It's very tasty, but vegetarian!

You can use veggies from your own garden, or locally grown produce to make it extra fresh.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Cheese
  • Assorted in-season vegetables
  • Tomato puree
  • Vegetable stock

Steps

  1. Bake the vegetables
  2. Layer them with the sheets and puree in a big tray
  3. Cover with cheese
  4. Bake for 40mins @ 180C
